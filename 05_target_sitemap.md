# Target sitemap

## Primary navigation

- Home
- Guide
- Map
- Saved
- More

Global Search appears in Home and Guide top bars. AI is a search action and contextual top-bar action, not a knowledge-owning tab.

## Guide

- Getting started
- Housing
- Official services
- Work and money
- Study
- Health and safety
- Transport
- Explore

Each category filters one shared canonical repository. Geography is an orthogonal facet: Country → Province → City → Place. Content types: Article, Official service, Place, City, Province, Checklist, Emergency action, External resource.

## More

- Profile and personalization
- Language
- Notifications
- Privacy and data
- Accessibility
- About, legal, feedback

More contains settings and product controls only; it does not duplicate the Guide library.

## Access rule

Audience tags Newcomer, Student, Visitor, Worker, Family and Resident affect order, badges and suggested filters only. Every public item remains reachable from Guide/Search/Map/contextual links.
