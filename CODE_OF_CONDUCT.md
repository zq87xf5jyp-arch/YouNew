# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

YouNew welcomes respectful, evidence-led participation. Contributors and
maintainers are expected to be considerate, constructive, and inclusive across
nationality, migration status, language, disability, age, gender identity,
religion, ethnicity, experience level, and other personal characteristics.

Examples of welcome behavior include:

- explaining technical or factual disagreements with reproducible evidence;
- respecting privacy, source rights, and the lived experience of newcomers;
- giving actionable feedback without personal attacks; and
- correcting mistakes openly and proportionately.

Harassment, threats, discriminatory language, sexualized attention, doxxing,
publishing personal data, deliberate misinformation, and sustained disruption
are not acceptable.

## Scope and enforcement

This policy applies in repository issues, pull requests, reviews, discussions,
and other spaces where someone represents the YouNew project.

Report conduct concerns privately to
[support@younew.nl](mailto:support@younew.nl). Do not include unnecessary
personal data. The maintainer may edit or remove content, close contributions,
restrict participation, or take other proportionate action. Reports will be
handled as confidentially as reasonably possible, subject to safety and legal
obligations.
