# How ChatGPT Fits the YouNew Workflow

## Owner-confirmed role and evidence boundary

The owner describes ChatGPT as a product and writing partner used to turn the
original YouNew idea into a clearer product, user journey, and public story. This
is an owner-supplied account of the collaboration, not a claim that ChatGPT made
the product decisions or authored the application.

The repository does not contain an exported ChatGPT conversation, dated model
badge, or commit-level attribution for individual ChatGPT sessions. It therefore
does not independently prove which specific idea, sentence, or design choice came
from a particular conversation. Public wording stays at the collaboration-role
level and avoids feature-level authorship or model-version claims.

## Transparent collaboration role

In the owner-described workflow, ChatGPT is the product, design, and content
reasoning space around implementation. Its role includes helping the owner:

- clarify the newcomer problem and the audience’s most important questions;
- compare product and information-architecture options;
- challenge whether a journey is understandable to someone new to the
  Netherlands;
- refine content structure, demo narration, and public project language; and
- turn engineering findings into owner decisions and the next bounded Codex task.

These points describe the owner-confirmed collaboration model, not a measured
contribution or independent authorship claim.

## Clear separation of roles

| Participant | Honest role in the Build Week workflow |
|---|---|
| Human owner | Sets vision and priorities, supplies context and references, accepts or rejects work, and owns rights, release, and submission decisions. |
| ChatGPT | Owner-confirmed reasoning collaborator for product, design, content, and narrative work; individual session details are not independently evidenced in the repository. |
| Codex | Engineering collaborator supported by repository artifacts for inspection, implementation, debugging, verification, and technical documentation. |
| YouNew in-app assistant | Deterministic local guided product functionality backed by structured workflows and indexed YouNew knowledge. It is not a ChatGPT session or verified live OpenAI inference. |

## Submission-safe wording

Use:

> I used ChatGPT as a product and writing partner and Codex as an engineering
> partner to turn a simple idea into a working iOS application. The vision,
> priorities, review, and final decisions remained mine.

Do not expand this into claims that ChatGPT authored specific source files,
generated governed content, used a particular model version, or produced a
measured percentage of the app. More specific historical claims would still need
dated, owner-approved evidence.

## What this does not claim

- That ChatGPT generated the YouNew source code or governed content.
- That a particular OpenAI model was used historically.
- That ChatGPT made final product or release decisions.
- That the YouNew app sends its demonstrated assistant flow to ChatGPT.
- That GPT-5.6 powers the current candidate.

The owner remains the decision-maker, and the candidate demo remains a local,
deterministic in-app experience.
