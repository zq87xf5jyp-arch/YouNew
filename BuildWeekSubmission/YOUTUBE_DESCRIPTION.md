## Title

YouNew — A clearer first step in the Netherlands | OpenAI Build Week

## Description

YouNew is a native SwiftUI guide for people moving to or settling in the
Netherlands. This demo follows one practical question—what comes after receiving
an address—and turns it into ordered BSN, DigiD, health-insurance and huisarts
next steps, a detailed guide, and a named official source.

The demonstrated in-app assistant is a deterministic local guided workflow
backed by structured YouNew content. It is not presented as live ChatGPT,
OpenAI, or GPT-5.6 inference. ChatGPT supported product development and writing;
Codex supported implementation, debugging, testing and technical preparation.
The product vision and final decisions remained human-directed.

Repository: https://github.com/zq87xf5jyp-arch/YouNew

YouNew is a Build Week demonstration candidate, not a government service,
professional advice, or a production-ready release. Consequential information
should be verified with the responsible official institution.
