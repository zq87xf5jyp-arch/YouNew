## Added content scope

Each priority city now has a newcomer guide structure covering municipality services, BSN/registration orientation, language and library support, healthcare orientation, hospital landmarks, legal and rights help, transport, community support, emergency/safety, documents/admin, LGBTQ+ support, and work/UWV orientation.

## Generic fallback

Other cities use a smaller reference structure: municipality services, library/language support, and emergency/safety. These are labelled as general guide/reference content unless an official municipality URL is available.

## Official sources linked

- Municipality websites where present in the existing province catalog
- Government.nl health insurance and 112 pages
- DigiD
- Juridisch Loket
- UWV
- City/library/transport operator sites for priority cities where known
- discriminatie.nl for discrimination reporting orientation

## Missing city data

- Exact office addresses are intentionally not stored unless already verified elsewhere.
- Opening hours are not stored.
- Phone numbers are not stored except the national emergency number 112 in explanatory text.
- City-specific family/children and housing support can be expanded after manual source review.

## Places needing manual verification

- Hospital landmarks are currently reference/general guide records unless an official source is added.
- Community/newcomer orientation cards are reference-only because local organizations vary by district and program.
- Library program details should be verified city by city before exposing specific class names or schedules.
